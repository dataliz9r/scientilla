Scientilla is our new platform designed to store and manage your research data in a quick and easy way!

Scientilla is designed for PIs, Researchers, Postdocs and PhD students to monitor their scientific production.

With Scientilla, you can certify and keep track of your research information. As a group leader, you will find the data of your team easily accessible and you can better design your strategy and reach your goals. And you can keep your scientific production up-to-date on your own.

Scientilla ensures the data driving our strategic decisions are trusted, comprehensive and accessible in real-time. By collecting in a single place all the research information, it offers a wider and more detailed view of the scientific activities of groups, research lines and areas.

Please use Scientilla with the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Opera, Safari or Edge.
