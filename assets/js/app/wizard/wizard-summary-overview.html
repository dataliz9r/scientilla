Details about the “Overview” tab


The plots in the “Overview” tab are based on all the verified documents in your profile.

- Plot “Documents by year”: it shows the number of documents according to their source type (where they are published): Journal, Conference, Book and Book series.

- Plot “Documents by type”: the pie chart shows the documents according to their document type, e.g. Article, Conference paper, Review, Note, ...

- Plot “Invited Talks by year”: it shows separately the invited talks hosted in Scientific and in Dissemination events. “Invited talks” are the events in which you have been invited to give a talk or a seminar, without previous submission of any abstract, paper or other document, for example: keynote speaker, teacher in a summer school or workshop, …
